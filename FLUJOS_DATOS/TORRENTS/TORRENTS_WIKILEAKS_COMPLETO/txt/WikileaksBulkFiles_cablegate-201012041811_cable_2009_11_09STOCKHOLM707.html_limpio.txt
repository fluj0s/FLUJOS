three years ago the swedish publics antius sentiments were strong but big shift has occurred president obamas election was one factor the other is better understanding by swedes of how to deal with the us she told the ambassador she would like to strengthen the usswedish relationship through cooperation on climate change and shared cultural values domestic politics sahlin said her party will have tough fight leading up to the 2010 general elections the most important issue will be jobs and the economy reftel especially as unemployment is very high and will continue to rise foreign policy issues will not decide the election outcome but discussion of swedens involvement in afghanistan could gain in volume compared to the rest of europe swedens xenophobic political party sweden democrats
