american law division updated february 22 1999 abstract this report traces the changes and derivation of the major amendments to the provisions of federal law authorizing the appointment of what is now called an independent counsel the original 1978 language of the law enacted as part of the ethics in government act of 1978 pl 95521 is provided in ﬁll in the lefthand column of the chart the entire text of the current provisions of law from the 1994 reauthorization pl 103270 is set out in the far righthand column of the chart the statutory text of signiﬁcant changes in the act is placed in the other columns corresponding to the major amendments and reauthorizations in which such language was ﬁrst adopted descriptions of some of the major changes are placed in the charts for
