masaya limited special resolution of the sole shareholder of masaya limited the company dated this day of 1999 having received recommendations from the sole director of the company it was resolved that the company cease trading immediately and that the company be liquidated and that the proceeds of the liquidation be paid to the sole shareholder it was further resolved that following the liquidation as the company will have assets or liabilities and will not be trading the company be dissolved and that the company secretary be instructed to inform the registrar of companies sole director
