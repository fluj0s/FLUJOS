international police liaison officers iraq assignment policy procedure manual 03700 housing effective 04152005 3700 purpose it is the purpose of this policy to inform international police liaison officers iplos of the housing standard in regards to their gender which is set in place to ensure an appropriate living environment while in theater 3701 applicabiliity to all cpatt iplos in every region regardless of the location facility fob etc or under whose operational control the site falls within 3702 policy all male and female iplos will be provided separated housing quarters regardless of the length of their stay at any site b it is the responsibility of the regional commander to ensure that every iplo is provided with separated housing in regards to the iplos gender at every fob within their region with exceptions 3703 implementation this policy is effective immediately b policy violations may result in severe disciplinary action to include termination 3704 exceptions the only exception to this policy will be in an emergency situation ie the site is under attack and then only for the duration of the emergency situation ________________________________ ________________________________ approved by paul middlemore approved by david dobrotka director of organizational division deputy commander cpatt cpatt pic
