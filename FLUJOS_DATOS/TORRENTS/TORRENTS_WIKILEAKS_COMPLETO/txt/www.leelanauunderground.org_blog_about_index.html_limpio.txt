about update february 14th 2009 leelanau talk is collection of students and alumni of the leelanau school that are concerned about the schools direction and practices we help insure transparency in school policies hold the school accountable to community members and provide safe place for student speech we work to directly and aggressively confront censorship on campus end the oppressive culture that is commonplace at leelanau educate students and staff about their rights increase awareness in members of the public about life at leelanau hold school administration and staff members accountable for their actions and words provide safe place for students and staff to speak emphasize direct action over appeals to administrators hold leelanau to their obligations as nonprofit organization obtain and disseminate information on leelanau especially when it would be of use to students potential donors parents or the media assist those who have been unfairly targeted by administrators and serve as an advocate it is worth emphasizing here that we do not mean to attack the faculty at leelanau many teachers and dorm parents have truly changed our lives for the better while there will always be faculty that we dont like those faculty will be called out individually comments yet name email will not be published website subscribe to comments feed almost there dates
