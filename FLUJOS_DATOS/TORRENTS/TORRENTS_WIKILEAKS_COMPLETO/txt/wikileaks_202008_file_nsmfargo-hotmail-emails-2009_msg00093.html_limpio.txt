injected bundled with the original source files this is very small little file that adheres to your computer at the kernel level hiding it effectively from antivirus and antispyware defense when players start the illegally downloaded versions of zogs nightmare 1 2 the software on computers that have an internet connection uses this connection to transmit the name of the computer being used and the name of the user signed in to the windows session most alarmingly the software also transmits the ip address of the players computer the nsmsdt will be investigating this matter in an attempt to analyze where the information is being sent some of our sources have suggested that the culprit may be the adl but this has not been confirmed we are urging all people that want to play zogs
