vilma hoy necesario nunca consagró toda vida luchar mujer cuba mayoría era discriminada ser humano igual resto mundo honrosas excepciones revolucionarias siempre así largo evolución histórica especie llevó ocupar papel social correspondía taller natural forja vida país mujer emergía horribles formas sociedad neocolonia yanqui bajo égida imperialismo sistema ser humano capaz crear sido convertido mercancía surgió lejana historia llamó explotación hombre hombre madres niños niñas desposeídos soportaron mayor carga mujeres cubanas trabajaban servicios domésticos tiendas lujo bares burgueses además eran seleccionadas cuerpo figura fábricas asignaban trabajos simples repetitivos peor remunerados educación salud servicios prestaban pequeña escala imprescindible cooperación era realizada maestras enfermeras solo ofrecían nivel medio preparación nación 12562 kilómetros extensión contaba únicamente centro educación superior ubicado capital adelante facultades centros universitarios 2 provincias norma solo podían estudiar jóvenes procedentes familias ingresos altos muchas actividades siquiera concebía presencia mujer he sido testigo casi medio siglo luchas vilma olvido reuniones movimiento 26 julio sierra maestra enviada finalmente dirección importante misión segundo frente oriental vilma inmutaba peligro alguno triunfar revolución inicia incesante batalla mujeres niños cubanos llevó fundación dirección federación mujeres cubanas hubo tribuna nacional internacional dejara asistir distante fuera camino recorrer defensa patria agredida nobles justas ideas revolución voz dulce firme oportuna siempre escuchó gran respeto reuniones partido organizaciones masas hoy mujeres cuba constituyen 66 ciento fuerza técnica país participan mayoritariamente casi todas carreras universitarias mujer apenas figuraba actividades científicas pues había ciencia científicos salvo excepciones campo hoy mayoría deberes revolucionarios inmenso trabajo nunca impidieron vilma cumplir struggles fidel castro ruz vilma is dead even though the news was expected it was still an impact out of respect for her delicate health condition i never raised her name in my reflections vilmas example today is more necessary than ever she devoted her entire life to the struggle for womens rights when in cuba most women were discriminated against as human beings the same as in the rest of the world with only the honorable revolutionary exceptions it was not always this way throughout the historical evolution of our species leading her to fulfill the social role befitting her as natural workshop where life is forged in our country women came out from under one of the most horrible forms of society that of yankee neocolony under the aegis of imperialism and its system where everything that the human being is capable of creating was turned into merchandise when what has been defined as the exploitation of man by man started far back in history the mothers and children of the dispossessed bore the brunt of the burden cuban women used to work as domestic servants or in luxurious shops and bourgeois bars selected for their good looks factories assigned them the simplest jobs the ones that were the most repetitive and worst paid in education and healthcare services provided on small scale their indispensable cooperation was as teachers and nurses who had only been offered basic training the country 200992 miles from end to end only had one higher education center located in the capital and later several faculties in university campuses in two other provinces as rule the only young women who could study there were those from the most affluent families in many activities the presence of woman was not even dreamed of for almost half century i have been witness to vilmas struggles i cannot forget her presence at the meetings of the july 26 movement in the sierra maestra she was eventually sent by the movements directorate to carry out an important mission on the second eastern front vilma did not shrink from any danger after the triumph of the revolution she began her ceaseless battle for the rights of cuban women and children which led her to found and lead the federation of cuban women there was national or international forum too distant for her to attend in defense of her assailed homeland and of the noble and just ideas of the revolution her gentle voice steady and timely was always listened to with great respect in party state and mass organization meetings today women in cuba make up 66 percent of the technical work force of the country and they take part in the main in almost all the university degree courses previously there were hardly any women involved in scientific activities since science and scientists did not exist but exceptionally in this field as well today women are in the majority revolutionary duties and her immense work load never prevented vilma from fulfilling her responsibilities as loyal wife and mother of several children vilma is dead long live vilma wwwaltercomorgarticle149376html this message is part of particular mailbox provided at wikileaks and it should be discussed here see also translate
