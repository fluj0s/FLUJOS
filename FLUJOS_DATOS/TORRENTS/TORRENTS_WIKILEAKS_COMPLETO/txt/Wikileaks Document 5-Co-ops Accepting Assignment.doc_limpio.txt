document 5 the board of uswaamamba keyeeri and shari rural cooperative societies accepting the assignment for the lease to silverdale mbono farms from mengis company fiona tanzania ltd to silverdale tanzania ltd the board of uswaamamba keyeeri and shari rural cooperative societies minutes of meeting of the board held at sal salinerohotel shanty town moshi at 12 pm on monday 10th may 2004 assignment of lease to silverdale t of silverdale and mbono farms it was noted that quorum was present the chairman noted that fiona t ltd had indicated that they were longer able to manage the above farms on their own and had requested that the board agree to their assigning the lease to silverdale t ltd it was resolved that the board accept this assignment and authorised the chairman and vice chairman to inform fiona t ltd and silverdale t ltd accordingly signed name chairman name vice chairman pic pic
